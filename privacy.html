Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 26, 2026

WTJ Content Studio ("we", "our", or "us") operates the WTJ Content Studio integration app. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with our TikTok API integration.

1. Information We Collect

Our application is an internal, self-hosted content publishing tool. We do not collect, store, or share any personal user data on external servers. The only data processed is the video content and metadata (such as captions) that you explicitly choose to upload to your own TikTok account.

2. How We Use TikTok API Data

We use the TikTok Content Posting API solely to upload videos and publish posts to your authorized TikTok account. The authentication tokens (OAuth tokens) are stored locally on your own secure computer and are never transmitted to third parties.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share any of your data, including data obtained via the TikTok API, with third parties. All operations are strictly local to your self-hosted instance.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We do not store your uploaded video files after the upload process is completed. You can revoke our application's access to your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok account settings. If you revoke access, the local access tokens stored on your machine will become invalid.
